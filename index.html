About This Work:

"Celestial Mechanics" is a Three.js-based digital universe that artfully blends the principles of physics with visual aesthetics within a simulated space environment. At the center of this interactive experience is a detailed, futuristic car that rotates to showcase its sleek design. Orbiting around this central car are three geometric forms—a sphere, a cube, and a cylinder—each tracing a path influenced by the car's gravitational pull. Additionally, upon each page load, a variety of shapes are randomly positioned and colored, each rotating independently. These shapes seem to drift effortlessly through space, adding a layer of unpredictability and movement that mimics celestial bodies in orbit. Dynamic lighting further enhances the visual depth and texture of the scene, creating a visually engaging and rich experience that captivates and inspires. To heighten the immersion, visitors can play "Tokyo Drift," adding an exhilarating auditory layer to the cosmic journey.
